About

E-Summit

The Entrepreneurship Summit, organised by the Entrepreneurship Development Cell of NIT Durgapur is usually held every year during the Entrepreneurship Week in the month of February. E-Summit is the second largest of it's kind in Eastern India. The mission of the summit is to educate and support student entrepreneurs, start-ups and SMEs for creating high-value jobs. This summit is organized not only in the interest of the students of NIT Durgapur but also for all those who are by and large interested in entrepreneurship related activities. E-Summit sees participation from multiple colleges throughout the country. Through E-Summit we hope to inspire and prepare the next wave of entrepreneurs who will know how to create and impact and keep on motivating .
